\section{Question}
Can you constrct the quadrilateral PLAN if
$PL = 6, LA = 9.5,\angle P = 75\degree
,\angle L = 150\degree
and
\angle A = 140\degree$
?
\section{solution}
The given information can be expressed as
\begin{align}\label{eq}
    \angle P=75\degree\\
    \angle L=150\degree\\
    \angle A=140\degree\\
    \norm{P-L}=6\\
    \norm{L-A}=9.5\end{align}
As we know the sum of the measures of the four angles of a quadrilateral is 360\degree\\
but here,$$\\\angle P+\angle L
+\angle A= 365\degree\\$$
hence it is not possible to construct the quadrilateral with above data.
